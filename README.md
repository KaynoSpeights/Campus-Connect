--- PROBLEM, TARGET USER, VALUE ---

Problem: Students living on WSSU campus without a vehichle have limited access to resources around town. Having to rely on Uber or friends for transportation can be a problem for some.
Target: By creating an app, that is for students, it allows some students to start their own entrepreneurship.
Value: This app allows students to get money in their pocket, and find resources close by without the need of a vehicle.


--- DATA & AI USE ---

Database of users who register businesses would be necessary along with users who create an account on the app to place orders/book appointments.
Ratings would be used to help display top performers for reliable service. AI could enhance this by quickly summarizing user reviews. 
An AI powered chatbot would enhance a user who may have questions about what a service is that is offered and the benefits of it. An example of this would be someone who focuses on making matchas and herbal teas. The health benefits that align with it is great information to get.
For the business side it can give recommendations for fair, competitive, prices. AI can also impact how the business owner analyzes their profits and suggest shifts in how to improve.
